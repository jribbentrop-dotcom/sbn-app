A **cadence** is a short chord progression that punctuates the end of a phrase.
The most conclusive is the *authentic cadence* (V–I), which resolves dominant
tension home to the tonic. A *plagal cadence* (IV–I) sounds softer, and a
*half cadence* ends on V, leaving the phrase open.
